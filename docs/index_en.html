Why Tramando

When you write a novel, you don't just manage text. You have characters with their traits, places with their atmospheres, interweaving themes, a timeline that must stay consistent. Tramando lets you connect everything.

Write a scene and link it to the characters who appear in it. Then, with one click, see all scenes where Elena appears, or how a theme develops throughout the story.

AI Assistant (optional)

Tramando includes AI integration to help you overcome creative blocks, explore phrase variants, or generate draft character sheets.

Completely optional and non-invasive. AI proposes, you always decide. No automatic changes to your text. If you're not interested, disable it and forget about it.
